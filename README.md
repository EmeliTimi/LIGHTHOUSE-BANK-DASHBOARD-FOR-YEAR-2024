Introduction

The Lighthouse Bank Churn Analysis Dashboard provides a data-driven view of customer attrition in 2024.
The dashboard analyzes churn behavior across age groups, gender, geography, and account balances to help the bank identify high-risk segments and improve customer retention strategies.












ðŸ“‚ Story of Data

The dataset represents simulated banking customer records designed to reflect real-world churn behavior.
Each record captures customer demographics, account status, and financial impact, enabling a holistic churn assessment.

Core fields include:

Customer Age Group

Gender

Country

Account Status (Active / Exited)

Account Balance

Churn Indicator















ðŸ”€ Data Splitting and Preprocessing

Before analysis, the data was prepared through:

Data cleaning and validation

Standardization of categorical variables (age groups, gender, country)

Separation of churned vs active customers

Aggregation of churn counts and churned balances

Creation of KPIs for total churn, average churned balance, and financial loss

Interactive visuals allowed quick drill-down into high-risk customer segments.
